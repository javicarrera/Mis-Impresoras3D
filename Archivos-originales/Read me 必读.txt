Firmware import machine
Step 1: Copy the following folders (2) and files (2) to TF and directory:
Mks_font (folder)
Mks_pic (folder)
Robin_nano43.bin
robin_nano43_cfg

Step 2: Insert the TF card into the machine and restart


Enable BLtouch:
Click "Settings"
Click on the "Machine Settings"
Click on the "Machine"
Click "Next"
Click on the "Leveling Settings"
Click on the "Leveling Settings"
Set "Enable Auto Leveling" to "Yes"
Set "Enable BLtouch" to "yes"

固件导入机器
步骤1：需要把以下文件夹（2个）和文件拷贝（2个）至TF根目录下：
mks_font（文件夹）
mks_pic（文件夹）
Robin_nano43.bin
robin_nano43_cfg
步骤2：把TF卡插入机器，重启


启用BLtouch：
点击“设置”  
点击“机器参数”
点击“机器设置”
点击“下一页”
点击“调平设置” 
点击“调平设置”
